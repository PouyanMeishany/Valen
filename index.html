What Keki does

Keki is a mobile marketplace that connects people who need everyday tasks done — moving help, cleaning, assembly, handywork, errands and more — with trusted local people ("taskers") who can do them.

  • Posters describe a task, set a budget, and review bids from nearby taskers.
  • Taskers browse open tasks, place bids, and get paid for completed work.
  • Messaging, identity verification, and secure payments are built into the app so both sides can transact with confidence.

Who runs Keki

Keki is operated by Pouyan Meishany, based in Sweden. We build and maintain the Keki app and platform that connects posters and taskers and facilitates payments between them.

Keki provides the platform; the actual tasks are performed by independent taskers, not by Keki itself.

What you're paying for

When a poster accepts a tasker's bid, the agreed amount is collected through our payment processor, Stripe, and held securely until the task is completed.

  • Posters pay for the service they requested — the price agreed with the tasker (plus any agreed material costs).
  • Funds are released to the tasker once the task is marked complete.
  • Keki may charge a small service fee for operating the platform and processing payments; any fee is shown before you confirm a payment.
  • We never see or store your full card details — all card data is handled directly by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment provider.

Refunds & cancellations

Cancelling before work begins

If a task is cancelled by either party before the tasker has started work, the held payment is refunded in full to the poster's original payment method.

Cancelling after work has started

If work has already begun, contact us and we'll help the poster and tasker agree a fair outcome — for example a partial refund for the portion of work not completed.

Disputes

If you're not satisfied with a completed task, raise a dispute in the app or email us within a reasonable time and we'll mediate between both parties.

How refunds are issued

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method via Stripe. They typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

Privacy policy

We respect your privacy and only collect what we need to run the service. This summary explains what we collect, why, and your rights.

Information we collect

  • Account details — name, email, phone number, and profile information you provide.
  • Task & location data — tasks you post or bid on, and approximate location used to match you with nearby tasks.
  • Identity verification — for taskers, a government ID and selfie are verified through Stripe Identity. Keki does not store the biometric data; it is processed by Stripe.
  • Payment information — handled by Stripe. We store only non-sensitive references (e.g. payment status), never full card numbers.
  • Messages exchanged between posters and taskers in the app.

How we use it

  • To operate the marketplace and match tasks with taskers.
  • To process payments and payouts, and prevent fraud.
  • To verify identity where required.
  • To provide support and communicate about your account.

Who we share it with

  • Other users, only as needed to complete a task (e.g. a poster and tasker can see each other's relevant details).
  • Service providers we rely on: Stripe (payments and identity verification) and Supabase (data hosting and storage).
  • Authorities, where we're legally required to.

We don't sell your personal data.

Your rights

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have rights under the GDPR — including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise any of these, email us at pouyan.meishany@gmail.com and we'll respond.
